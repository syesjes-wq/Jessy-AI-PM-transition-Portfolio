# Decision Log

## Decision 001

### Question

How should finding correctness be evaluated?

### Observation

No benchmark existed for validating AI-generated findings.

### Action

Investigated Stockfish as a potential benchmark.

### Outcome

Discovered that Stockfish provides move-level evidence rather than parent-facing findings.

### Decision

Stockfish alone could not serve as the benchmark.

---

## Decision 002

### Question

How can finding correctness be evaluated consistently?

### Observation

A shared definition of a valid finding did not exist.

### Action

Created a finding taxonomy.

### Outcome

Finding correctness became traceable and evaluable.

### Decision

Use taxonomy as an evaluation artifact.

---

## Decision 003

### Question

How should AI-generated findings be assessed?

### Observation

Finding correctness and evidence sufficiency were being treated as the same thing.

### Action

Created a Finding Evaluation Framework.

### Outcome

Correctness and evidence quality could be evaluated independently.

### Decision

Separate Evidence Verification from Finding Validation.
